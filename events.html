FrostHacks 2025


Dive into a day of creativity, innovation, and problem-solving as you tackle challenges related to winter science, technology, and engineering.

Join us for Frost Hacks 2025, a one-day winter-themed hackathon/ideathon designed exclusively for high school students from Evanston and the surrounding towns! Whether you're a coding enthusiast or a budding designer, there’s something for everyone. Choose from a variety of challenges—some requiring coding skills and others focusing on design and problem-solving. Enjoy engaging bootcamps throughout the day to enhance your skills, network with peers, and have fun in a collaborative environment.
